Question

In picture a) the paper is divided into two equal parts, each piece is half of the original. This fraction is written as 1⁄2 and read as ‘one-half’.

Think about how many parts the paper is divided into in picture b) and how you would write one piece as a fraction.

View the answer
The paper would be divided into four equal parts, each piece is a forth of the original. This fraction is written as 1⁄4 and read as 'one-forth'.
