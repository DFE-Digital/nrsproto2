Adding and subtracting fractions

If you were to add 1⁄2 and 1⁄3 it is hard to picture what the answer would be. Rewriting the fractions with a common bottom number, or denominator makes it easier.

To add or subtract fractions:

  • turn any mixed numbers into improper fractions
  • change the fractions so they have the same denominator
  • add or subtract the numerators
